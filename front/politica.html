Política de Privacidad

The email will be stored in the company's account if the user decides to contact us through Gmail. Spam and junk mail will not be sent. Only the user's email will be answered. The information provided will not be shared with third parties outside the company. All information is stored responsibly. No tracking, cookies, or other similar technologies are used. No sensitive information or personal data beyond email is collected for direct communication. There are no other personal data collection or use practices at this time. Users will be notified of any changes via an alert on the website's homepage. For more information, contact us at info@ng-globalpartners.com or visit our LinkedIn profile.
