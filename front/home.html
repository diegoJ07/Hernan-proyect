Our People

Mr. Hernan Novoa he is Managing Partner of NG Global Partners with more than 20 years of experience working in top-tier institutions. Previously, he spent 7 years working in JP Morgan as Senior Vice President for LatAm covering regional and global initiatives across all lines of business, before that he spent 6 years in BNP Paribas covering Capital Markets initiatives for Argentina. He also worked in PWC as Senior Consultant in charge of projects across Europe and North America and he was in Citi and Banco Hipotecario in Argentina. He holds a Postgraduate in Finance from Pontifical Catholic University of Argentina (UCA) and Certified Public Account from University of Buenos Aires (UBA).

Mr. Maximiliano Galli is Managing Partner of NG Global Partners with a background in Finance for over 20 years, he also sits in the board of two private companies in Argentina and he is a professor at Universidad Catolica Argentina. He has worked with Deloitte, PwC, Central Bank of the Republic of Argentina, Central Bank of Economic Integration, Puente and Economía & Regiones. He was also an advisor to sub-national governments in Argentina. He has assisted financing project finance in Central America. He has been involved in renewable energy, oil, gas and mining deals in Argentina. He holds a Postgraduate degree in Finance from Universidad Torcuato Di Tella and Certified Public Account from University of Buenos Aires (UBA).
