Overview

How We work

NG Global Partners is a consulting boutique firm with a global reach. We select the projects we will assist. Once we understand the project’s risk and return balance and confirm we are aligned with the developer's visions and values, we carefully look for the right equity & debt partner.

assemble a robust team between developers and sources of funds working through out the entire financial close. We engage and work with architects, constructors, financial advisors, real estate agents and lawyers trying to craft the best deal possible for everyone.

Our People

Our People

Our team is comprised of seasoned professionals and entrepreneurs with solid backgrounds in different countries & companies across the region.

Mr. Hernan Novoa

He is Managing Partner of NG Global Partners with more than 20 years of experience working in top-tier institutions. Previously, he spent 7 years working in JP Morgan as Senior Vice President for LatAm covering regional and global initiatives across all lines of business, before that he spent 6 years in BNP Paribas covering Capital Markets initiatives for Argentina. He also worked in PWC as Senior Consultant in charge of projects across Europe and North America and he was in Citi and Banco Hipotecario in Argentina. He holds a Postgraduate in Finance from Pontifical Catholic University of Argentina (UCA) and Certified Public Account from University of Buenos Aires (UBA).

Mr. Maximiliano Galli

Is Managing Partner of NG Global Partners with a background in Finance for over 20 years, he also sits in the board of two private companies in Argentina and he is a professor at Universidad Catolica Argentina. He has worked with Deloitte, PwC, Central Bank of the Republic of Argentina, Central Bank of Economic Integration, Puente and Economía & Regiones. He was also an advisor to sub-national governments in Argentina. He has assisted financing project finance in Central America. He has been involved in renewable energy, oil, gas and mining deals in Argentina. He holds a Postgraduate degree in Finance from Universidad Torcuato Di Tella and Certified Public Account from University of Buenos Aires (UBA).
