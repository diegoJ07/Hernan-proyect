HOW WE WORK

NG Global Partners operates as a boutique strategic advisory firm with a global reach, selectively supporting high-quality projects. We work closely with developers to align vision and objectives and provide strategic advisory support by leveraging our network of established relationships to facilitate introductions to appropriate capital and financing counterparties.

Throughout the process, all parties retain full responsibility for their own decisions, risks, and legal considerations. NG Global Partners acts solely in a strategic advisory capacity.

OUR VALUES

Exceptional Service

We are committed to providing honest, transparent, and timely feedback, supporting each project through strategic advisory and thoughtful analysis aligned with our client’s objectives.

Long Term Relationships

We believe in building strong, sustainable relationships based on trust and collaboration.

Integrity

We conduct our business in accordance with the highest ethical and professional standards.

OUR PEOPLE

Hernán Novoa – Founder & Managing Partner, Has over 20 years of experience in strategic advisory, capital markets, and financial services across leading global institutions. Prior to founding NG Global Partners, he spent seven years at JPMorgan as Senior Vice President for Latin America, where he oversaw regional and global initiatives across multiple business lines. Previously, he led Capital Markets initiatives for Argentina during his six-year tenure at BNP Paribas. Earlier in his career, he was a Senior Consultant at PwC, managing advisory engagements across Europe and North America, and held roles at Citi and Banco Hipotecario in Argentina. He holds a Postgraduate Degree in Finance from the Pontifical Catholic University of Argentina (UCA) and is a Certified Public Accountant from the University of Buenos Aires (UBA).
